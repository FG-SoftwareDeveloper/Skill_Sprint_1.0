Mechatronics 2 is On Its Way

Dive deeper into robotics and automation. Build complex, intelligent systems and master the skills needed for advanced mechatronics engineering.

What to Expect

This course builds on the foundations of Mechatronics 1, taking you into the advanced world of robotics, automation, and intelligent system design.

Advanced Robotics with ROS

Learn to program complex robotic systems using the Robot Operating System (ROS), the industry standard for robotics development.

SLAM & Navigation

Explore Simultaneous Localization and Mapping (SLAM) algorithms to allow your robots to autonomously navigate and build maps of their environment.

Advanced Control Systems

Dive into PID controllers, Kalman filters, and other advanced control theory to build highly stable and precise mechatronic systems.

Sensor Fusion & Perception

Integrate data from multiple sensors (LiDAR, cameras, IMUs) to give your systems a robust understanding of their surroundings.

Machine Learning Integration

Discover how to use machine learning models on edge devices to enable your projects to make intelligent decisions in real-time.
